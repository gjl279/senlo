About Us

SENLO

Senlo Construction takes pride in breathing new life into domestic and commercial properties. With more than ten years of experience, ​​our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering high-quality ​workmanship; and innovative design solutions that enhance the value and appeal of your property.

  • DESIGN :   the property is based on your unique requirements and gives you a complete idea of what your finished renovation will look like. 
  • HIGH-QUALITY :  we work closely with our clients to choose high-quality materials from bathroom suites to paint colors to compliment your newly renovated property.
  • PROFEESIONAL : we renovate your property by our professional experts who have more than ten years of trade experience in their specific areas.
  • FLEXIBILITY :  we offer flexible renovation solutions tailored to our clients' unique needs and preferences.
  • RELAX : our project manager will manage everything  from concept to completion.

Senlo was born out of a passion for creating a beautiful property for our clients. With over 10 years of experience in construction and renovation, we decided to bring the whole package.
